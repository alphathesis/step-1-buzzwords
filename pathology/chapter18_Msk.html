  • achondroplasia

    AD activating mut in FGFR3, impaired cartilage prolif in growth plate --> poor endochondral bone formation (long bones, cartilage matrix then replace with bone)
    assn inc paternal age
    short extremities with nml head and chest (nml intramembranous bone b/c no cartilage matrix- flat bones of skull and ribcage)

  • osteogenesis imperfecta

    AD defect in type 1 collagen synthesis, structurally weak bone
    multiple fractures (mimic abuse, no bruises), blue sclera (choroidal veins), hearing loss (middle ear bones)

  • osteopetrosis

    abnl thick heavy bone that fractures, poor osteoclast function (carbonic anydrase II mutation= loss of acidic env)
    bone fractures, pancytopenia with extramedullary hematopoesis b/c of myelophthisic process, hearing/visual impaired from CN impingement, hyrocephalus from narrow foramen magnum, type 2 RTA from carbonic anhydrase mutation (met acidosis)
    tx with BM txp

  • Vitamin D deficiency

    osteoblast osteoid mineralized with Ca and phosphate
    low vit D = low Ca, low Phosphate --> defective mineralization
    active vit D from sunlight exposure, 25-OH in liver, 1a-OH in kidney
    functions= inc Ca/Phos absorption, inc Ca/Phos reabs in kidney, inc bone resorption
    deficiency due to dec sun exposure, malabsorption, liver failure, kidney failure
    Rickets- low vit D in kids <1y/o
    pigeon breast, frontal bossing from skull osteoid deposition, rachitic rosary from costochondral osteoid deposition, bowing of legs
    osteomalacia- low vit D in adults, weak bone with inc fracture risk
    dec Ca, dec phos, inc PTH, inc ALP

  • osteoporosis

    dec trabecular bone mass --> porous bone with inc fracture risk
    peak bone mass at 30y/o due to genetics/diet/exercise
    rate of loss due to diet/exercise/estrogen
    MC is senile and postmenopausal
    bone pain and gractures in vertebrae (dec height, kyphosis), hip, distal radius
    DEXA scan to measure bone density
    all labs nml (exclude osteomalacia)
    tx with exercise/vitD/Ca to dec loss, bisphosphonates for osteoclast apoptosis, estrogen debates, glucocorticoids contraindicated

  • paget dz of bone

    osteoclast/osteoblast imbalance, avg age 60, localized
    3 phases- osteoclastic, mixed, osteoblastic
    thick sclerotic bone with mosaic pattern of lamellar bone, fractures easily
    bone pain from microfractures, inc hat size b/c skull, hearing loss from nerve impingement, lion-facies from craniofacial obnes, ISOLATED ELEVATED ALP
    tx with calcitonin (inhib osteoclast), bisphosphonate (osteoclast apoptosis)
    can lead to high-output CHF from bone AV shunts, osteosarcoma

  • osteomyelitis

    infx, usu children, MC bacterial (hematogenous spread)
    kids=metaphysis, adults=epiphysis
    MCC staph aureus, N.gonorrhoeae in sexually active YA, Salmonella in sickle cell, pseudomonas in DM and IVDA, pasturelly in cat/dog bite/scratch, M.TB in Pott dz vertebrae
    bone pain with fever and leukocytosis, lytic focus/sequestrum with surrounding sclerosis/involucrum
    dx by blood culture

  • avascular/aseptic necrosis

    ischemic necrosis
    from trauma, steroids, sickle cell (dactylitis), caisson dz (gas emboli)
    causes osteoarthritis and fracture

  • Osteoma

    benign bone tumor, MC on facial bone surface, assn gardner syndrome (FAP+fibromatosis in retroperitoneum+facial bone osteoma)

  • osteoid osteoma

    benign tumor of osteoblasts with rim reaction, YA<25 y/o, mlaes, diaphysis of long bones
    bone pain that resolves with aspirin
    bony mass <2cm with radiolucent osteoid core

  • osteoblastoma

    >2cm, vertebrae, bone pain does NOT respond to aspirin

  • osteochondroma

    MC benign bone tumor, tumor with overlying cartilage cap, lateral projection from metaphysis growth plate
    continuous with marrow space

  • osteosarcoma

    malignant prolif of osteoblasts, teens and elderly
    RF= familial retinoblastoma (Rb gene), Paget dz, radiation
    long bone metaphysis (distal femur or proximal tibia- knee)
    pathologic fracture or bone pain with swelling
    imaging shows sunburst appearing, lifting of perosteum, Codman triangle
    biopsy has large pleiomorphic cells that produce pink osteoid

  • giant cell tumor

    multinucleated giant cells and stromal cells, YA, long bone epiphysis (distal femur or proximal tibia- knee), soap-bubble on xray, locally aggressive

  • Ewing sarcoma

    malignant, male <15 y/o, poorly diff cells from neuroectoderm, long bone diaphysis, onion-skin on xray, biopsy with small round blue cells with t(11:22), mets common, responds to chemoTx

  • chondroma

    benign, cartilage, medulla of small bones of hands/feet

  • chondrosarcoma

    malignant, cartilage, medulla of pelvis of central skeleton

  • mets to bone

    more common than primary, osteolytic lesions, prostate CA causes osteoblastic lesions

  • Joint

    connection btwn 2 bones
    solid joints- tightly connected for structural strength
    synovial joints- space for motion, articular surface made of type 2 collage/hyaline cartilage, joint capsule with synovium lining (fluid rich in hyaluronic acid for lubrication)

  • Osteoarthritis (degenerative joint dz)

    MC arthritis, progressive degen of articular cartilage from use
    RF= age, obesity, trauma
    hips, lower lumbar, knees, DIP, PIP
    morning stiffness that worsens with use
    biopsy shows joint mice (disrupted cartilage fragments in joint space), eburnation of bone, osteophyte formation (DIP Heberden nodes, PIP Bouchard nodes)

  • rheumatoid arthritis

    chronic systemic AI dz, women of childbearing age, assn HLA-DR4
    synovitis leads to pannus (inflammed granulation tissue), destruction of cartilage and ankylosis/fusion of joint
    morning stiffness that improves with use
    symmetric PIP swan-neck, wrists radial deviation, elbows, ankles, knees (NO DIP)
    joint space narrowing, loss of cartilage, and osteopenia on xray
    rheumatoid nodules in skin/viscera with central necrosis surrounded by epitheliod histiocytes
    vasculitis
    baker cyst (swollen bursa behind knee)
    pleural effusion, LAN, interstitial lung fibrosis
    labs have rheumatoid factor (IgM against Fc of IgG), neutros and protein in synovial fluid
    leads to anemia of chronic dz and secondary amyloidosis (SAA-->AA)

  • seronegative spondyloarthropathies

    no rheumatoid factor, axial skeleton, assn HLA-B27
    ankylosing spondyloarthritis- sacroiliac joints and spine, young males, low back pain, bamboo spine (vertebral body fusion), uveitis and aortitis (regurg)
    Reiter syndrome- triad arthritis/urethritis/conjunctivitis, male YA after GI or chlamydia infx
    Psoriatic arthritis- 10% of pts with psoriasis, axial and peripheral joints, MC DIP of hands and feet (sausage fingers/toes)

  • infectious arthritis

    usu bacterial, N.gonorrhoeae in YA (MC), staph aureus in children and adults
    single joint (knee)
    warm joint with limited ROM, fever, inc WBC, inc ESR

  • gout

    monosodium urate crystals, overproduction or dec excretion of uric acid (purine metabolism produces, kidney excretes)
    MC is primary with unknown etiology
    secondary is from leukemia (inc cell turnover), Lesch-Nyhan (X-linked HGPRT deficiency, MR and self mutilation), or renal insufficiency
    acute is extremely painful great toe (podagra) with MSU crystal deposition and exacerbation by EtOH/meat
    chronic has chalky white tophi of uric acid crystal with fibrosis and giant cell rxn, urate nephropathy
    labs show hyperuricemia, synovial fluid with needle-shaped crystals with yellow negative birefringence

  • pseudogout

    calcium pyrophosphate dihydrate deposition, synovial fluid with rhomboid-shaped crystals with weak positive birefringence

  • Dermatomyositis

    inflammatory dz of skin and skeletal muscle, assn gastric carcinoma
    bilateral proximal weakness (combing hair, climbing stairs), heliotrope eyelid rask, malar rask, gottron papules on elbows/knuckles/knees
    inc CK, ANA+, anti-Jo Ab
    biopsy with peripheral perimysial CD4 and perifascicular atrophy, tx with corticosteroids

  • Polymyositis

    inflammatory dz of skeletal muscle, no skin, endomysial CD8 inflammation with necrotic muscle fibers

  • X-linked muscular dystrophy

    dystrophin mutation (Duchenne= absent, Becker=mutated), muscle wasting, replace muscle with adipose tissue
    proximal muscle weakness, gower sign, calf pseudohypertrophy, inc CK
    death from cardiac/respiratory failure

  • Myasthenia gravis

    AutoAB against postsynaptic AChR, women, muscle weakness that improves with REST, involves eyes (ptosis, diplopia), sx improve with AChE inhibitors, assn thymoma

  • Lambert-Eaton

    AB against presynaptic Ca channels, assn small cell carcinoma paraneoplastic syndrome, impaired ACh release, muscle weakness improves with USE, eyes spared, AChE inhibitors DO NOT improve sx, improve with CA resection

  • Lipoma

    benign, adipose tissue, MC benign soft tissue tumor in adult

  • liposarcoma

    malignant, adipose tissue, MC malignant soft tissue tumor in adult, lipoblasts

  • rhabdomyoma

    benign, skeletal muscle, cardiac rhabdomyoma assn with tuberous sclerosis

  • rhabdomyosarcoma

    malignant, skeletal muslce, MC malignant soft tissue tumor in kids, desmin+ rhabdomyoblast, MC in head/neck, Young girls in vagina
