  • microcytic anemia

    MCV <80, dec HgB- iron deficiency, ACD, thalassemia, sideroblastic

  • iron deficiency

    low ferritin, high TIBC, low serum iron, low % sat of transferrin, inc FEP
    dietary lack or BLOOD LOSS

  • ACD

    hepcidin = sequester iron, low ferroportin
    high ferritin, low TIBC, low serum iron, low % sat, inc FEP
    treat underlying cause

  • alpha thalassemia

    gene deletion, cis worse than trans, 3 genes deleted= HBH, 4 deleted = HB barts which is lethal in utero

  • beta thalassemia

    gene mutation (pt mut in promotor/splice sites)
    minor= diminished, target cells, low HbA with inc HbA2 and HbF
    major is severe- alpha tetramers cause hemolysis, extramedullary hematopoesis, crewcut, aplastic crisis with parvo B19, target cells and nucleated RBCs, little to no HbA with high HbA2 and HbF

  • sideroblastic

    defective protoporphyrin synthesis (lead inhibits ALAD and ferrochelatas, EtOH is mito poison, vit B6 is cofactor for ALAS)
    high ferritin, low TIBC, high serum iron, high % sat
    iron-laden macs = ringed sideroblasts

  • Macrocytic anemia

    MCV >100, B12/folate defieciency, dec divisions b/c dec THF so less thymidine so DNA synthesis impaired

  • folate deficiency

    abs in jejunum, small stores run out in couple months, hypersegmented nucs, glossitis, inc homocysteine, nml MMA

  • B12 deficiency

    R binder in saliva --> panc proteases detach in duodenum --> bind intrinsic factor from parietal --> abs in ileum
    large stores so takes years
    pernicious anemia most common
    neurologic Sx!
    inc homocystein and MMA

  • normocytic anemia

    dec production (dec RPI) or hemolysis (inc RPI)

  • EVH

    HS, sickle cell, HbC
    anemia with splenomegaly, jaudince from unconj bilirubin, bilirubin gallstones, marrow hyperplasia

  • hereditary spherocytosis

    RBC cytoskeleton ankyrin/spectrin/band3.1 defect, blebs formed and lost, consumed by splenic macs, spherocytes with loss of central palor, osmotic fragility test, splenectomy causes howell-jolly bodies

  • sickle cell

    AR mut of beta chain, missense point mutation, HbS polymerizes when deoxy, EVH and IVH, extramedullary hematopoiesis, vaso-occlusion crises, metabisulfite screen detects trait

  • HbC

    AR mut of beta chain (glutamic acid to lysine), HbC crystals

  • warm IgG AIHA

    IgG warm agglutinin, spherocytes, assn with SLE/CLL/PCN/cephalosporins, central body, Coombs test

  • IVH

    destruction within vessels, Hgbnemia/Hbguria/hemosiderinuria, dec haptoglobin
    PNH, G6PD deficiency, cold IgM AIHA, microangiopathic hemolytic anemia, malaria

  • cold IgM AIHA

    igM fixes complement in cold extremities, mycoplasma pneumoniae and infx mono, Coombs test

  • PNH

    acquired defect in GPI leads to defect in DAF (inhibits C3 convertase to protect RBC), IVH at night b/c mild resp acid activates complement, sucrose test to screen, flow cytometry to detect CD55, thrombosis is cause of death b/c released contents induce thrombosis

  • G6PD deficiency

    X-lined, dec half-life of G6PD --> dec NADPH --> can't reduce glutathione --> oxidative injury
    mediterranean worse than african
    oxidative stress = heinz bodies (infx, drugs, fava beans), bite cells

  • malaria

    plasmodium transmitted by female anopheles, RBC rupture as part of life cycle
    cylclical fever falciparum=everyday, vivax/ovale=every other day

  • underproduction anemia (low RC)

    parvo b19 (infects progenitor RBC), aplastic anemia (fatty marrow, pancytopenia), mylophthisic process (myelofibrosis or CA in marrow causes pancytopenia), renal failure (dec EPO)
