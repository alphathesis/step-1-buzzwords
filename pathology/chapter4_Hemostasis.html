  • primary hemostasis

    transient vasocontriction (reflex neural and endothelin), plt adhesion (vWf from WP bodies binds subendo collagen, GPIb binds vWF), plt degranulation (ADP, TXA2), plt aggregation (GP!!b/IIIa, fibrinogen)

  • primary hemostasis dz

    mucosal/skin bleeding, epistaxis, hemoptysis, petechiae/purpura/ecchymoses, easy bruising, thrombocytopenia, nml PT/PTT

  • ITP

    IgG from spleen plasma cells against GPIIb/IIIa and plt consumed by splenic macs, dec plt count, inc megakaryocyte

  • microangiopathic hemolytic anemia

    plt microthrombi in small vessels consume plt, schistocytes b/c RBC sheared
    TTP (ADAMTS13 dec so inc vWF), HUS (O157:H7 ecoli infx or drugs), DIC, HELLP in pregnant, prosthetic valves
    thrombocytopenia with inc bleed time

  • bernard-soulier

    GPIb deficiency- impair adhesion

  • glanzman thrombasthenia

    GPIIb/IIIa deficiency- impair aggregation

  • ASA

    irreveribly inactivate COX- dec TXA2 so dec aggregation

  • uremia

    dec adhesion and aggregation

  • secondary hemostasis

    stabilize plt plug via coag cascade, thrombin converts fibrinogen to fibrin, crosslink, factor 7 (extrinsic, PT, thromboplastin/tissue factor), factors 12/11/9 (intrinsic, PTT, subendo collagen)

  • secondary hemostasis dz

    deep tissue bleeding (hemarthrosis), rebleeding after surgery, altered PT/PTT

  • hemophilia

    A (8)- x-linked, inc PTT, PTT corrects on mixing
    B (9)

  • coag factor inhibitor

    acquired Ab, anti-8 most common, PTT doesn't correct on mixing

  • VWF dz

    most common is AD with dec vWF level- inc bleed time (dec adhesion), inc PTT b/c dec factor 8 half-life, abnl ristocetin test, desmopressing= inc vWF release from WP bodies

  • vit K deficiency

    gamma carboxylation of 2,7,9,10 and proteins C,S
    activated by epoxide reductase in liver

  • liver failure

    dec factor production, first inc PT then inc PTT (7 has shorted half-life)

  • HIT

    secondary to heparin Tx (Pf4 --> IgG --> splenic consumption), stop heparin and give another anticoag (not warfarin)

  • warfarin skin necrosis

    thrombosis due to dec protein c/s (shortest half life)

  • DIC

    widespread microthrombi from OB/sepsis/adenoCA/ApML/rattlesnake, bleeding from IV sites and mucosal bec plt consumed, dec plt count, inc PT/PTT, dec fibrinogen, inc D-dimer (fibrin split product)

  • fibrinolysis

    tPA (plasminogen --> plasmin), plasmin cleaves fibrin, alpha-2 antiplasmin inactivates plasmin
    dz- urokinase from radical prostectomy (activate plasmin) or liver cirrhosis (dec antiplasmin), inc PT/PTT, inc bleet time with nml plt count, no D-dimers

  • thrombosis

    virchow triad- disrupt blood flow (stasis, turbulence), endothelial damage (atherosclerosis, vasculitis, homocysteine- vit b12/folate or CBS deficiency), hypercoag state (protein c/s deficiency, factor V leiden, prothrombin 20210A, ATIII deficiency, oral contraceptives/estrogen)

  • embolism

    atherosclerotic, fat (long bone), gas (decompression), amniotic fluid

  • PE

    from DVT (femoral, iliac, popliteal), dyspnea, pleuritic chest pain, V/Q mismatch, spiral CT, D-dimer elevated, hemmorhagic wedge infarct, sudden death with saddle embolus, pulm HTN in chronic emboli

  • systemic emboli

    from left heart
