  • pituitary adenoma

    benign, anterior pituitary
    nonfunctional- no hormones produced, mass effect (bitemporal hemianopsia, hypopituitarism, headach)
    prolactinoma- female with galatorrhea/amenorrhea or male with dec libido/headache
    MC pit adenoma
    tx with dopamine agonist (bromocriptine, cabergoline)
    GH cell adenoma- gigantism in kids (linear growth with unfused epiphyses)
    agromegaly in adults (hands/feet/jaw, visceral organs, tongue- MCC death is cardiac failure)
    secondary DM (inc liver gluconeogenesis)
    elevated GH and IGF-1 and lack of suppresion with oral glucose
    tx with octreotide (somatostatin analogue- dec GHRH effect on AP)
    ACTH cell adenoma- cushing syndrome
    TSH, LH, FSH adenomas- rare

  • hypopituitarism

    insufficient hormone production, sx when >75% parenchyma lost, due to adult pit adenoma/ kids craniopharyngioma (mass effect) or pituitary apoplexy (bleeding into adenoma)
    sheehan syndrome- pit gland doubles in size during pregnancy but blood supply constant, blood loss during parturition causes pituitary infarction
    poor lactation, lose pubic hair, fatigue
    empty sella syndrome- congenital defect, herniation of arachnoid/CSF into sella compresses pit gland, no pit gland on imaging

  • Posterior pituitary

    ADH and oxytocin storage (made in hypothalamus), also neurophysin

  • central diabetes insipidus

    ADH deficiency, hypothalamic or post pit pathology, loss of free water (polyuria, polydipsia, dehydration, hypernatremia, inc serum osm, low urine osm), water deprivation test does NOT inc urine osm, tx with desmopressin

  • nephrogenic diabetes insipidus

    impaired renal response to ADH, inherited mutation or lithium/demeclocycline, no response to desmopressin

  • SIADH

    excessive ADH, MC ectopic production (small cell CA in lung), also CNS trauma/pulm infx/cyclophosphamide, retention of free water (hyponatremia, low serum osm, neuronal swelling and cerebral edema), tx with water restriction and demeclocycline

  • thyroglossal duct cyst

    cystic dilation of thyroglossal duct remnant, failure to involute after thyroid descent, anterior neck mass

  • lingual thyroid

    persistent thyroid tissue at base of tongue, mass at base of tongue, failure to descend

  • Thyroid function

    I- uptake via active txp --> oxidation and organification by peroxidase --> TBG with bound hormone --> endocytosis --> salvage iodine, release T4>T3

  • hyperthyroidism

    inc TH --> inc basal metabolic rate (Na/K pump) and inc symp NS activity (inc beta-1 rcptrs)
    weight loss, inc appetite, heat intolerance, sweating, tachycardia, arrhythmia, tremor/anxiety/insomnia, staring gaze with lid lag, diarrhea, oligomenorrhea, bone resorption with hyperCa, hypoXOL, hyperglycemia (gluconeogenesis and glycogenolysis)
    Graves dz- MCC hyperthyroidism, AutoAb IgG that stimulates TSHr (type 2 HSR), women of childbearing age, diffuse goiter (constant TSH stimulation=thyroid hyperplasia and hypertrophy), exophthalmos (fibroblasts with TSHr), pretibial myxedema (glycosamineglycan buildup, inflam, fibrosis, edema), irregular follicles with scalloped colloid
    inc T4, dec TSH (inc T3 downregulates TRHr in AP), hypoXOL, inc glucose
    tx with beta-blocker, thioamide (peroxidase inhib), radioiodine ablation
    THYROID STORM (inc catecholamines and massive hormone excess from stress --> arrhythmia, hyperthermia, vomiting, hypovolemic shock
    tx with PTU, BB, steroids)

  • multinodular goiter

    enlarged thyroid with multiple nodules, from relative iodine deficiency, nontoxic/eutyroid, rarely some regions become TSH-independent and cause hyperthyroidism

  • cretinism

    hypothyroidism in neonates/infants
    MR, short stature with abnl skeletal, coarse facial, enlarged tongue, umbilical hernia
    due to maternal hypothyroidism, thyroid agenesis, dyshormogenetic goiter (congenital thyroid peroxidase defect), or iodine deficiency

  • myxedema

    hypothyroidism in children/adults
    myxedema (glycosamineglycan in skin/soft tissue, deep voice, large tongue), weight gain with nml appetite, slow mental, muscle weakness, cold intolerance, bradycardia with dec CO causes SOB, oligomenorrhea, hyperXOL, constipation
    caused by iodine deficiency, Hashimoto, lithium or radioablation

  • Hashimoto thyroiditis

    AI thyroid destruction, MCC hypothyroidism, assn HLA-DR5
    initial hyperTH then hypoTH, antithyroglobulin and antithyroid peroxidase AB
    chronic inflamation with germinal center and HURTHLE CELLS, inc risk marginal B-cell lymphoma (enlarging thyroid)

  • De Quervia/subacute granulomatous thyroiditis

    follows viral infx, tender thyroid with transient hyperTH, self-limited, 15% get hypoTH

  • Reidel fibrosing thyroiditis

    chronic inflam and fibrosis, HARD AS WOOD thyroid, NONTENDER, young females

  • thyroid neoplasia

    distinct solitary nodule, benign>malignant, I-131 radioactive uptake study (Hot= graves, nodular
    cold= adenoma, carcinoma), biopsy with FNA (no cutting b/c extensive blood supply)

  • follicular adenoma

    benign prolif of follicles, fibrous capsule, usu nonfunctional

  • papillary carcinoma

    MC type thyroid CA (80%), due to ionizing radiation in childhood (acne), papillae with clear ORPHAN ANNI EYE NUCLEI and nuclear grooves, psammoma bodies of concentric calcification, spread to cervical LN, excellent Px

  • follicular carcinoma

    malignant prolif of follicles, fibrous capsule, invasion through capsule (distinction cannot be made with FNA), hematogenous mets

  • medullary CA

    malignant prolif of PARAFOLLICULAR C CELLS, secrete calcitonin (hypoCa, deposits as amyloid), malignant cells in AMYLOID STROMA
    familial cases in MEN2A/B (RET mutation- prophylactic thyroidectomy)

  • anaplastic CA

    undifferentiated malignant tumor, elderly pts, invades local structures (dysphagia, resp compromise), HARD AS WOOD thyroid, poor px

  • parathyroid

    chief cells make PTH --> inc bone osteoclast via osteoblast activation (inc resorption= inc Ca, inc phosphate), inc Ca and Phosphate absorption (via Vit D), inc renal Ca reabs and dec phosph reabs

  • hyperparathyroidism

    primary- Parathyroid adenoma MCC (benign), asymptomatic hypercalcemia, calcium oxalate nephrolithiasis, nephrocalcinosis, CNS dep/seizures, constipation, peptic ulcer dz, acute pancreatitis (Ca activates enzymes), osteitis fibrosa cystica (bone resorption)
    inc PTH, inc Ca, dec phosphate, inc urinary cAMP, inc serum ALP
    secondary- MCC is chronic renal failure (dec phosphate excretion)
    inc serum phospate, dec Ca, inc PTH, inc ALP (renal osteodystrophy)

  • hypoparathyroidism

    low PTH
    due to AI, surgical or DiGeorge
    low Ca sx (numbness and tingling, tetany/muscle spasm- Trousseau sign, Chvostek sign)
    dec PTH, dec Ca

  • pseudohypoparathyroidism

    end-organ resistance to PTH
    hypoCa with inc PTH
    AD form has short stature with short 4th/5th digits

  • Endocrine pancreas

    Islets of Langerhans, central beta cells make insulin (anabolic, upregulates GLUT4 on muscle/adipose, dec serum glucose, glycogen/protein/lipo synthesis), peripheral alpha cells make glucagon (glycogenolysis, lipolysis)

  • type 1 DM

    AI destruction of beta cells by T-cells, islet inflammation, AB against insulin, seen in childhood
    assn HLA-DR3/4
    high serum glucose (low insulin), weight loss and low muscle mass (unopposed glucagon), polyuria/polydipsia/glycosuria (osmotic diuresis)
    tx with insulin
    risk for DKA (excessive serum ketones with stress (epi --> glucagon --> lipolysis --> FFAs --> liver makes ketone bodies
    glucose >300, AG met acidosis, hyperkalemia, kussmaul respiration, mental status change, fruity breath from acetone
    tx with fluid, insulin, potassium)

  • type 2 DM

    end-organ insulin resistance
    MC type 90%
    middle-aged obese adults (obesity= dec insulin rcptrs), stronger genetic predisposition
    early high insulin then low insulin from beta exhaustion
    amylin deposits in islets
    polyuria/polydipsia/hyperglycemia
    random glucose >200, fasting glucose >126, glucose tolerance test >200 2hr after loading
    tx with lifesyle mod, sulfonylurea/metformin, exogenous insulin
    risk for hyperosmolar NONKETOTIC coma (glucose >500 causes diuresis with hypoTN and coma, no ketones b/c low insulin level)

  • DM consequences

    NEG of vasc BM- atherosclerosis in large/med vessels (CV dz, peripheral vasc dz requiring amputation), hyaline arteriolosclerosis in small vessels (renal glomerulosclerosis of efferent- nephrotic syndrome with Kimmelstiel-Wilson nodules), NEG of HgB= HbA1c
    Osmotic damage- glucose freely enters Schwann cells, pericytes of retinal BVs, lens
    aldose reductase makes SORBITOL
    peripheral neuropathy, impotence, blindness, cataracts

  • endocrine pancreas tumors

    islet cell tumor- <5%, MEN1 (panc, pit, parathyroid)
    insulinoma- episodic hypoglycemia with mental status change, relieved by glucose admin
    dec glucose, inc insulin, inc C peptide
    gastrinoma- Zollinger ellison syndrome, tx-resistant peptic ulcers, can extend into jejunum
    somatostatinoma- achlorhydria (inhibit gastrin --> dec HCl secretion), cholelithiasis with steatorrhea (inhibit CCK)
    VIPoma- watery diarrhea, hypokalemia, achlorhydria

  • Adrenal cortex

    Glomerulosa- mineralocorticoid (aldo), Fasciculata- glucocorticoid (cortisol), Reticularis- sex steroids (testosterone)

  • Hypercortisolism (Cushing syndrome)

    excess cortisol
    muscle weakness (muscle breakdown), moon facies/buffalo hump/truncal obesity (high glucose --> fat storage), abdominal striae (impaired collagen), HTN, osteoporosis, immune suppression
    dx with 24hr urine cortisol
    high-dose dexamethasone test differentiates pit ACTH from ectopic ACTH
    exogenous corticosteroids- bilateral adrenal atrophy, suppress ACTH secretion
    primary adrenal adenoma/hyperplasia/carcinoma- atrophy of uninvolved adrenal
    ACTH pit adenoma- CUSHING DISEASE, bilateral adrenal hyperplasia, cortisol levels dec with high-dose dexamethasone test
    paraneoplastic ACTH- small cell CA of lung, bilateral adrenal hyperplasia, cortisol levels remain high with high-dose dexamethasone test

  • Hyperaldosteronism (Conn syndrome)

    excess aldo, HTN with Na/H2O retention, hypokalemia, met alkalosis
    primary hyperaldo- sporadic adrenal hyperplasia MC, high aldo, low renin
    secondary hyperaldo- RAA activation from CHF or renal hypoperfusion, high aldo, high renin

  • congenital adrenal hyperplasia

    21-hydroxylase deficiency: MC CAH, dec aldo and dec cortisol, shunting causes inc sex steroids (clitoral enlargement in female, precocious pubery in males)
    salt wasting and life-threatening hypoTN
    dec cortisol --> inc ACTH --> bilateral adrenal hyperplasia
    11-hydroxylase deficiency: dec aldo and dec cortisol, shunting causes inc sex steroids, no salt wasting (weak mineralocorticoid produced)
    17-hydroxylase deficiency: dec cortisol and dec sex steroids, hyperaldosteronism

  • Adrenal insufficiency

    lack of adrenal hormones
    Waterhouse-Friderichsen syndrome: acute insufficiency, hemorrhagic necrosis due to DIC in kids with N.meningitidis
    dec cortisol exacerbates hypoTN
    Addison dz: chronic due to progressive destruction
    AI, TB, mets from lung
    hypoTN, hyponatremia, hypovolemia, hyperkalemia, weakness, HYPERPIGMENTATION (inc ACTH)

  • adrenal medulla

    neural crest derived chromaffin cells, special sympathetic ganglion, catecholamine source (NE and epi)

  • pheochromocytoma

    chromaffin cell tumor (inc catecholamines --> HTN, headache, palpitations, tachycardia, sweating)
    inc serum metanephrines, inc urine 24hr metanephrine and VMA (metabolites)
    phenoxybenzamine then surgical excision
    rule of 10s (10%- bilateral, familial, malignant, outside of adrenal medulla)
    assn MEN2A/B, VHL, neurofibromatosis type 1
    extra-adrenal sites: bladder wall (HTN with urination), organ of Zuckerkandl at IMA root
