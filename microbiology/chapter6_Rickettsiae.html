  • Rickettsia rickettsii

    OBLIGATE INTRACELLULAR, need CoA and NAD+, no giemsa stain, Ag cross-rxn with OX proteus vulgaris (Weil-Felix), invades endothelial lining of caps
    from DERMACENTOR DOG TICK, causes ROCKY MTN SPOTTED FEVER (southatlantic USA, rask starts on wrist/ankles then to trunk/palms/soles), Dx from Ab using ELISA
    tx with tetracycline

  • Rickettsia prowazekii

    OBLIGATE INTRACELLULAR, no giemsa stain, Ag cross-rxn with OX proteus vulgaris (Weil-Felix)
    human reservoir, BODY LOUSE bite, causes TYPHUS (rash starts centrally, no palms/soles)
    tx with tetracyline, vax for military

  • Rickettsia typhi

    OBLIGATE INTRACELLULAR, no giemsa stain, Ag cross-rxn with OX proteus vulgaris (Weil-Felix),from FLEAS, causes ENDEMIC TYPHUS

  • Ehrlichia chaffeensis

    Rickettsiae, southern USA
    from LONE STAR TICK, causes human monocytic ehrlichiosis (MORULAE IN MONOCYTE CYTOPLASM), rash is rare

  • Anaplasma

    Rickettsiae
    from IXODES TICK, MORULAE IN GRANULOCYTE CYTOPLASM, rash is rare

  • Coxiella burnetii

    OBLIGATE INTRACELLULAR, NOT RICKETTSIA but related, no giemsa stain
    from cattle/sheep (inhale urine/feces/amniotic fluid/placenta), NO ARTHROPOD VECTOR, causes Q FEVER with pneumonia sx, no rash
    tx with tetracycline, killed vax for high-risk occupations
