  • Neisseria meningitidis

    G- KIDNEY-BEAN DIPLOCOCCI, oxidase+, MALTOSE fermenter, ENCAPSULATED- antiphagocytic, endotoxin causes septic shock, IgA protease
    colonize upper resp tract, reach meninges via blood
    causes meningitis, meningococcemia, WATERHOUSE-FRIEDRICHSEN SYNDROME
    culture on chocolate agar
    tx with PCN-G
    prevent with capsular polysacharride A/C/Y/W-135 vax or polysacharride coupled to diptheria toxoid (no vax for type B)
    rifampin or ciprofoxacin for close contacts

  • Neisseria gonorrhoeae

    G- KIDNEY-BEAN DIPLOCOCCI, oxidase+, no capsule, no maltose, weaker endotoxin, IgA protease, PILI
    STD- gonorrhea and PID, also neonatal conjunctivitis, septic arthritis, fitz-hugh-curtis syndrome
    culture on THAYER-MARTIN, diplococci seen within neutros
    tx with cefriaxone plus tetracycline for Chlamydia urethritis, resistant to PCN from penicillinase or dec perm/altered PBP
    treat newborn eyes with erythromycin or silver nitrate
    no vax b/c antigenis variation
    use condoms, erythromycin ointment to prevent neonatal transmission

  • Escherichia coli

    G- rod, facultative, LACTOSE fermenter, endotoxin (LPS) causes septic shock, K1 capsular for neontal meningitis, fimbrae for cystitis/pyelonephritis
    causes UTI, sepsis, neonatal meningitis
    colon flora, UTI risk in women from close proximity and short urethra, also anatomical abnl and indwelling catheters
    culture on EMB or MacConkey's agar, TSI agar with ACID SLANT/ACID BUTT
    tx with ampicillin or sulfonamide for UTI 3rd gen cephalosporin for meningitis/sepsis, rehydration for traveler's diarrhea
    has beta-lactamase and aminoglycoside-modifying enzymes
    prophylactic doxycycline or pepto bismol for traveler's diarrhea

  • EIEC

    invades intestinal mucosa --> necrosis, inflammation

  • ETEC

    watery traveler's diarrhea
    LT enterotoxin= ADP ribosylates AC --> inc cAMP --> Cl and H2O flow out, ST enterotoxin stimulates GC
    no inflammation, no invasion

  • EPEC

    pediatric diarrhea
    no toxin, adheres to apical, flattens villi, prevent absorption

  • O157:H7 EHEC

    no sorbitol
    verotoxin (shiga-like)- remove adenine from 28S rRNA --> inhib prot syn, bloody diarrhea, HUS (anemia, thrombocytopenia, ARF)
    from undercooked meat

  • Salmonella typhi

    G- rod, facultative, no lactose, H2S, endotoxin=fever, capsule Vi antigen
    causes typhoid fever
    ONLY HUMAN, infects reticuloendothelial system (liver/spleen), chronic carrier state in GB, dec stomach acid predisposes
    rose spots on abd, fever, headache, diarrhea
    culture on EMB or MacConkey's agar, TSI with ALKALINE SLANT/ACID BUTT
    tx with ceftriaxone, ampicillin/TMP-SMX for less severe, resistance to chloramphenicol by acetylating enzymes, beta-lactamase
    purified Vi polysaccharide capsule or live vax available

  • Salmonella enteitidis

    G- rod, facultative, no lactose, H2S, MOTILE (flagella), 1500 serotypes, inactivated by stomach acid, only endotoxin/no exotoxin
    invates and causes MONOCYTIC RESPONSE, disseminate via blood
    many animal reservoirs, enteric flora, large infectious dose required
    causes enterocolitis, sepsis with metastatic abscesses, sickle cell osteomyelitis
    culture on EMB or MacConkey's, TSI with ALKALINE SLANT/ACID BUTT with GAS
    tx with ceftriaxone for sepsis, abx prolong fecal excretion
    acetylating enz (chloramphenicol resistance), beta-lactamase (ampicillin resistance)

  • Shigella

    G- rod, facultative, no lactose, NONMOTILE, endotoxin
    human colon flora, NO BLOOD SPREAD, invades ileum/colon mucosa to cause PMN IFILTRATION, low infectious dose, outbreaks in institutions/day care
    causes enterocolitis/dysentery with BLOODY DIARRHEA
    culture on EMB or MacConkey's, TSI with ALKALINE SLANT/ACID BUTT
    tx with fluid/electrolytes, ciprofloxacin for severe, resistance via beta-lactamase and mutant pteroate synthetase (sulfonamides)

  • Yersina enterocolitica

    G- rod, no lactose
    domestic animal, transmission via fecal contamination of food, causes enterocolitis like shigella/salmonella, also mesenteric adenitis

  • Vibrio cholerae

    G- COMMA rod, oxidase+, GROWS IN ALKALINE, A/B enterotoxin- ADP ribosylate Gs --> AC --> inc cAMP --> Cl/H2O outflow, mucinase enhances attachment
    human colon flora and shellfish, high infectious dose, causes cholera (RICE-WATER DIARRHEA)
    tx with fluid/electrolytes, tetracycline shortens duration and used in close contacts
