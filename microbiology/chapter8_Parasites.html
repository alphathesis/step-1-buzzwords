  • Entamoeba histolytica

    intestinal protozoan, motile trophozoite that multiply in colon, cysts with 4 nuclei (ingest cysts in water and trophozoites form in small intestine), cysts pass in feces
    fecal oral, trophozoites invade colon epithelium (FLASK SHAPED ULCER if submucosal abscess ruptures), can spread to liver
    cause amebic dystentery (bloody diarrhea) and liver abscess with anchovy paste exudate and RUQ pain
    dx with trophozoites/cysts in stool, indirect hemagglutination test for liver dz
    tx with metronidazole/tinidazole fo sx, iodoquinol/paromomysin for asymptomatic cysts carriers

  • Giardia lamblia

    intestinal protozoan, pear-shaped, flagella, cysts with 4 nuclei (ingest cysts in water, form trophozoites in duodenum, cysts pass in feces)
    fecal oral, human and animal, trophozoites DO NOT invade wall, interfere with fat/protein absorption
    causes giardiasis (bloating, flatulence, foul-smelling fatty diarrhea)
    dx with trophozoite/cysts in stool, STRING TEST
    tx with metronidazole
    prevent with water purification, handwashing

  • Cryptosporium parvum

    intestinal protozoan, ingest oocysts in water --> oocysts release sporozoites --> form trophozoites --> schizonts and merozoites --> microgametes and macrogametes --> zygote --> oocyst
    fecal oral, human and animal, trophozoites DO NOT invade wall
    cause cryptosporidiosis (diarrhea)
    dx with OOCYSTS in stool with ACID-FAST stain
    paromomycin for sx

  • Trichomonas vaginalis

    urogenital protozoan,pear-shaped, flagella, trophozoites, NO CYSTS OR OTHER FORMS
    STD, trophozoites attach to wall of vagina and cause inflammation/discharge (GREEN FOUL-SMELLING, ITCHING, BURNING)
    dx with MOTILE trophozoites in secretions, STRAWBERRY CERVIX
    tx with metronidazole for both partners
    condoms to prevent

  • Plasmodium

    blood/tissue protazoan
    gametogony in humans, sporogony in mosquitoes, schizogony in humans
    sporozoites in femal ANOPHELES mosquito saliva enter human blood --> invade hepatocytes (exoerythrocytic), form merozoites/hypnozoites --> merozoites infect RBC (ANEMIA) --> form schizonts and release more merozoites (CYCLIC FEVER- malariae every 3 days, vivax/ovale/falciparum 2 days) --> some merozoites become gametocytes, ingested by female anopheles, gametes form zygote then oocyst with sporozoites
    tropical Asia, Africa, Latin America
    causes malaria (FEVER, HEADACHE, ANEMIA, SPLENOMEGALY)
    falciparum causes RBC aggregates that occlude caps (TISSUE ANOXIA- BRAIN, KIDNEY), hypnozoites cause relapse
    dx with blood smear (TROPHOZOIT RING WITHIN RBC, SCHIZONT CONTAINING MEROZOITES)
    tx with chloroquine for sensitive, mefloquine/quinine+doxycycline for falciparum, primaquine for hypnozoites, TEST FOR G6PD DEFICIENCY BEFORE QUINIDINE IV
    prevent with chloroquine/mefloquine/doxycycline, control mosquitoes

  • Toxoplasma gondii

    tissue protozoan, ingest cysts in raw meat or oocysts in cat-feces --> differentiate in gut and invade gut wall --> trophozoites/tachyzoites in macs --> cysts containing bradyzoites
    trophozoites pass transplacentally if primary infx during pregnancy
    trophozoites infect brain/eyes/liver, form large cysts, severe dz in immunocompromised (BRAIN ABSCESS IN HIV), congenital toxoplasmosis triad (CHORIORETINITS, HYDROCEPHALUS, INTRACRANIAL CALCIFICATIONS)
    tx with sulfadiazine+pyrimethamine for congenital or disseminated
    prevent by cooking meat, avoid cat handling if pregnant

  • Pneumocystis jiroveci

    respiratory pathogen, YEAST with protozoan features, inhaled
    causes pneumonia, inflammation in alveoli
    dx with SILVER STAIN
    tx with TMP-SMX

  • Trypanosoma cruzi

    blood/tissue protozoan, trypomastigotes in reservoir blood ingested by REDUVIID BUG --> form epimastigotes then trypomastigotes --> feces with trypomastigotes contamines PAINLESS bite wound --> form amastigotes in RBC (kill cells= MYOCARDITIS, neuronal damage causing megacolon/megaeso), amastigotes become trypomastigotes to continue cycle
    rural latin america, causes CHAGAS DZ (dilated CM, megacolon, megaeso)
    dx with trypomastigotes in blood
    tx with nifurtimox/benznidazole for acute dz, no tx for chronic

  • Trypanosoma brucei (gambiense/rhodesiense)

    blood/tissue protozoan, trypomastigotes in reservoir blood ingested by TSETSE FLY --> form epimastigotes in gut then metacyclic trypomastigotes in salivary glands --> trypomastigotes enter blood with PAINFUL bite --> infect brain (ENCEPHALITIS), repeated antigen variation (evade immune response)
    Gambiense in west Africa with human reservoir, Rhodesiense in east Africa with antelope reservoir
    causes African Sleeping Sickness (enlarged LN, recurring fever, somnolence, coma)
    dx with trypomastigotes in blood early and in CSF late
    tx with suramin for early dz, suramin+melarsoprol for CNS sx

  • Leishmania donovani

    blood/tissue protazoan, human macs with amastigotes ingested by SANDFLY --> form promastigotes in fly gut which migrate to pharynx --> fly bite transmits promastigotes, enter macs and form amastigotes, can infect liver/spleen
    animal reservor in Africa/Middle East/China, human reservoir in India
    causes Kala azar (VISCERAL leishmaniasis- SPIKING FEVERS, HSM, PANCYTOPENIA), amastigotes kill reticuloendothelial cells (liver/spleen/BM), dx with amastigotes in BM smear
    tx with sodium stibogluconate/Amphotericin B

  • Leishmania tropica/mexicana/braziliensis

    causes mucocutaneous leishmaniasis, transmitted by sandflies, dx with amastigotes in skin lesion smear, tx with soidum stibogluconoate

  • Acanthamoeba castellanii

    causes meningoencephalitis and keratitis in pts with contact lens
    trophozoite and cyst stages
    freshwater lakes and soil
    transmitted via skin/eye trauma
    dz in immunocompromised pts
    dx with ameba in spinal fluit
    tx with pentamidine/ketoconazole/flucytosine

  • Naegleria fowleri

    causes rapidly fatal meningoencephalitis
    FRESHWATER lakes and soil
    trophozoite and cysts stages
    transmitted when swimming/diving
    dz in healthy ppl
    dx with ameba in spinal fluid
    tx with amphotericin B

  • Babesia microti

    causes babesiosis (FEVER, HEMOLYTIC ANEMIA)
    endemic in northeast US rodents --> transmitted by IXODES ticks
    infect RBC --> lysis (ANEMIA), severe dz in asplenic pts
    dx with MALTESE CROSS TETRAD IN RBC
    tx with quinine/clindamycine combination

  • Balantidium coli

    CILIATED protozoan, causes diarrhea, fecal oral from domestic animals (pigs)
    dx with trophozoites or cysts in feces
    tx with tetracycline

  • cyclospora cayetanensis

    COCCIDIAN protozoan, causes diarrhea in immunocompromised pts, fecal oral, no animal reservoir
    dx with OOCYTES in ACID-FAST stain of fecese
    tx with TMP-SMX

  • isospora belli

    COCCIDIAN protozoan, causes diarrhea in immunocompromised pts, fecal oral, human/animal reservoir
    dx with OOCYTES in ACID-FAST stain of fecese
    tx with TMP-SMX

  • microsporidia- Enterocytozoon bieneusi, Septata intestinalis

    SPORE-FORMING OBLIGATE-INTRACELLULAR protozoan, cause diarrhea in immunocompromised, fecal oral, humans
    dx with SPORES WITHIN CELLS in feces
    tx with albendazole

  • Diphyllobothrium latum

    cestode, fish tapeworm, scolex has 2 elongated sucking grooves, gravid uterus forms rosette, oval eggs with operculum at end
    ingest undercooked fish with sparganum larvae --> attach to gut wall and become adults with gravid progottids --> eggs passed in feces, eggs hatch in freshwater and embryos eaten by copepods --> copepods eaten by freshwater fish, larvae form in fish muscle
    humans are definitive hosts, endemic in Scandinavia/Japan/north and central US
    causes VITAMIN B12 DEFICIENCY (ANEMIA)
    dx with eggs in stool
    tx with praziquantel

  • Echinococcus granulosus

    cestode, dog tapeworm, scolex has 4 suckers and double circle of hooks, adult worm has 3 proglottids
    dogs infected by eating sheep liver with hydatid cysts --> form adults in gut, eggs passed in feces --> eggs ingested by sheep/humans, hatch hexacanth larvae in gut --> larvae migrate to LIVER/brain forming LARGE UNILOCULAR HYDATID CYSTS with protoscoleces and daughter cysts
    humans are deadend host, endeming in mediterranean/middle east/western US
    cyst rupture can cause ANAPHYLAXIS from antigens in fluid
    tx with albendazole or cyst removal

  • Taenia saginata

    cestode, beef tapeworm, scolex has 4 suckers, no hooks, gravid proglottids with 15-20 uterine branches
    humans ingest undercooked beef with cysticerci --> larvae attach to gut and become adults with gravid proglottids --> terminal proglottids detach and pass in feces --> eaten by cattle, oncosphere embryos hatch in cow gut --> buttow into blood vessels, migrate to skeletal muscle, form cysticerci
    humans are definitive host, endemic in Asia/latin america/eastern europe
    cyticercosis DOES NOT occur
    dx with gravid proglottids in stool
    tx with praziquantel

  • Taenia solium

    cestode, pork tapeworm, scolex has 4 suckers and circle of hooks, gravid proglottids with 5-10 uterine branches
    humans ingest undercooked pork with cysticerci --> larvae attach to gut and become adults with gravid proglottids --> terminal proglottids detach and pass in feces --> eaten by pigs, oncosphere embryos hatch in pig gut --> buttow into blood vessels, migrate to skeletal muscle, form cysticerci
    humans are definitive host, endemic in Asia/latin america/southern europe
    cyticercosis DOES occur with mass lesions in BRAIN
    dx with gravid proglottids in stool
    tx with praziquantel, albendazole for neurocysticercosis

  • Schistosoma

    trematode, blood fluke
    2 adult sexes but attached to each other, EGGS WITH SPINES
    cercariae infect human SKIN --> form larvae --> penetrate BVs and carried to liver --> become adults in liver, adult flukes migrate retrograde in portale vein (PORTAL HTN) or urinary bladder venules --> eggs penetrate gut/bladder wall, excreted --> hatch in freshwater --> ciliated larvae penetrate SNAILS and multiply to form free cercariae
    humans are definitive hosts, endemic in tropical (mansoni in Africa/latin america, japonicum in Asia, haematobium in Africa/Middle East)
    eggs in tissue indue inflam/granulomas/fibrosis/obstruction, mansoni damages colon (inf mesenteric venules), japonicum damages small int (sup mesenteric venules), haematobium damages bladder (predispose to squamous CA with painless hematuria)
    dx by eggs in feces/urine
    tx with praziquantel

  • Clonorchis sinensis

    trematode, liver fluke
    humans ingest undercooked FISH with encysted larvae --> immature flukes enter biliary duct in duodenum, become adults, release eggs --> eggs passed in feces --> snails ingest eggs --> eggs hatch and form miracidia --> multiply and form free cercariae which encyst under fish scales
    humans are definitive host, endemic in Asia
    causes BILIARY TRACT inflammation (PIGMENTED GALLSTONES, assn cholangioCA)
    dx by eggs in feces
    tx with praziquantel

  • Paragonimus westermani

    trematode, lung fluke
    humans ingest undercooked freshwater CRAB meat with encysted larve --> immature flukes enter peritoneal cavity from gut, burrow through diaphragm into lung parenchyma, become adults --> eggs enter bronchioles and are coughed/swallowed --> eggs hat in freshwater and release miracidia --> enter snales and multiply to form cercariae that encyst in crabs
    humans are definitive host, endemic in Asia/India
    causes inflammation and secondary bacterial infx of lung
    dx by eggs in sputum/feces
    tx with praziquantel

  • Ancylostoma duodenale/ Necator americanus

    intestinal nematode, HOOKWORM
    filariform larvae penetrate SKIN of feet --> enter blood and migrate to lungs --> enter alveoli and pass up trachea --> swallowed --> become adults in small int, attach to walls via teeth/cutting plates --> eggs pased in feces --> form noninfx rhabditiform larvae and infx filariform larvae in soil
    only humans, endemic in tropics
    causes ANEMIA from GI blood loss
    dx with eggs in feces and eosinophilia
    tx with mebendazole or pyrantel pamoate

  • Ascaris lumbricoides

    intestinal nematode, ASCARIASIS
    humans eat food contaminated with eggs in soil --> form larvae in gut, larvae migrate through blood to lungs --> enter alveoli, pass up trachea, swallowed --> become adults in gut, eggs passed in feces --> embryonate in soil
    only humans, endemic in tropics
    larvae in lung can cause pneumonia, can cause intestinal obstuction/malnutrition
    dx with eggs in feces and eosinophilia
    tx with mebendazole or pyrantel pamoate

  • Enterobius vermicularis

    intestinal nematode, PINWORM
    humans ingest eggs --> become adults in gut --> females migrate from anus and lay eggs on skin at night --> embryo becomes infective larva in 4-6 hr
    causes PERIANAL PRURITUS
    dx by SCOTCHTAPE TEST for eggs, adult worms in diaper
    tx with mebendazole or pyrantel pamoate

  • Strongyloides stercoralis

    intestinal nematode, STRONGYLOIDIASIS
    filariform larvae in soil penetrate skin, enter blood, migrate to lung --> move into alveoli and up trachea, swallowed --> becomes adults and enter mucosa --> females make eggs that hatch in colon into noninfx rhabditiform larvae that are passed in feces --> form filariform larvae in soil
    sometimes rhabditiform larvae molt in gut and form infx filariform larvae (enter blood, migrate to lung - AUTOINFX)
    endemic in tropics
    causes vomitting/diarrhea/epigastric pain similar to PUD, massive superinfx in immunocompromised
    dx by larvae in stool and eosinophilia
    tx with ivermectin or thiabendazole

  • Trichinella spiralis

    intestinal nematode, TRICHINOSIS
    humans eat undercooked pork with encysted larvae --> mature into adults in small int --> females release larvae that enter blood, migrate to SKELETAL MUSCLE AND BRAIN
    humans are deadend hosts, endemin in eastern europe and west africa
    causes larvae encysting and striated muscle NURSE CELLS, inflammation of muscle
    dx by encysted larvae in muscle biopsy and eosinophilia
    tx with thiabendazole for early, steroids+mebendazole for severe sx

  • Trichuris trichiura

    intestinal nematode, WHIPWORM
    humans ingest food/water contaminated with eggs in soil --> become adults in gut --> eggs passed in feces into soil
    only humans, tropics
    dx with eggs in feces, tx with mebendazole

  • Dracunculus medinensis

    tissue nematode, DRACUNCULIASIS
    humans ingest copepods with infx larvae in water --> larvae released in gut, migrate to body cavity and mature/mate --> fertilized female migrates to subcutaneous tissue and forms ULCERATING PAPULE --> motile larvae released into water, eaten by copepods
    humans are definitive host, endemic in tropical africa/Middle East/India
    tx with thiabendazole/metronidazole and extract worm from skin ulcers
    prevent by water purification

  • Loa loa

    tissue nematode, LOIASIS
    deer fly (mango fly) bit deposits infective larvae --> crawl into skin, become adults, migrate subcutaneously --> females make microfilariae that enter blood --> ingested by deer flies, form infx larvae
    only human definitive host, endemic in central/west africa
    hypersensitivity to worm causes SKIN SWELLING, adult worm crawls across conjunctiva
    dx by microfilariae in blood smear
    tx with diethycarbamazine

  • Onchocerca volvulus

    tissue nematode, RIVER BLINDNESS
    female blackfly bite deposits larvae in subcutaneous skin (HYPERPIGMENTED) --> mature into adult worms within skin nodules --> female make microfilariae that migrate in interstitial fluid --> ingested by blackflies, form infx larvae
    endemic along rivers of tropical africa and central america
    Microfilariae in eye can cause blindness, adult worms cause inflammatory skin nodules (scaly dermatitis= lizard skin), loss of subcutaneous tissue= hanging groin
    dx by microfilariae in skin biopsy, NOT BLOOD
    tx with ivermectin for microfilariae, suramin for adult worms

  • Wuchereria bancrofti

    tissue nematode, FILARIASIS
    female mosquito bite deposits infx larvae into bite wound --> become adults and produce microfilariae --> circulare in blood at night, ingested by mosquitoes
    endemic in tropical areas
    adult worms cause inflammation that blocks lymphatic vessels (ELEPHANTIASIS) from chronic repeated infx, sx 9-12mo after bite
    dx with microfilariae on blood smear
    tx with diethylcarbamazine for microfilariae

  • Toxocara canis

    nematode larvae cause VISCERAL LARVA MIGRANS
    ingest eggs in food/water contaminated with dog feces --> hatch into larvae in small int --> enter blood and migrate to LIVER/BRAIN/EYES --> trapped and die in organ
    humans are deadend hosts
    granulomas around dead larvae, granulomas in retina causes blindness
    dx with larvae in tissue
    tx with albendazole/mebendazole

  • Ancylostoma canium/braziliense

    filariform larvae of dog/cat hookwork causes CUTANEOUS LARVA MIGRANS
    larvae in soil burrow through skin --> migrate within subcutaneous tissue, CREEPING ERUPTIONS pruritic rash
    tx with thiabendazole

  • Anisakis simplex

    larvae cause ANISAKIASIS
    larvae ingested in raw seafood (sashimi/sushi) --> migrate into intestinal submucosa
    acute infx mimics appendicitis

  • Lice- Pediculus humanus, Phthirus pubis

    PEDICULOSIS
    transmitted by contact, lice easily visible, eggs/nits on hair shaft or clothing
    causes itching due to hypersensitivity rxn to louse saliva
    tx with permethrin

  • Flies- Dermatobia hominis

    MYIASIS
    Dermatobia deposits egg on mosquito --> mosquito bit deposits eggs on skin --> skin warmth causes hatching and larva enters skin
    larvae/maggots cause inflammatory response
    tx by surgical removal of larva

  • Mites- Sarcoptes scabiei

    SCABIES
    mites have round body with 8 short legs, too small to be seen
    transmitted by contact
    itching due to hypersensitivity rxn to mite feces
    tx with permethrin

  • Ticks- Dermacentor

    TICK PARALYSIS
    neurotoxin in tick saliva enters bite site --> blocks release of ACh (similar to botulinum toxin)
    tick removal reverses paralysis

  • Black widow- latrodectus mactans

    spider bite, orange-red hourglass on ventral surface
    neurotoxin causes pain in extremities/abdomen, numb/fever/vomitting
    tx with antivenom for severe

  • Brown recluse- loxosceles reclusa

    spider bite, violin-shaped pattern on dorsal surface
    dermotoxin is protease, causes painful necrotic lesions
    no antivenom
