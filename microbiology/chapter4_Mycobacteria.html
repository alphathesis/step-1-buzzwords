  • Mycobacterium tuberculosis

    ACID FAST- mycolic acid and wax D cell wall, aerobic, CATALASE+ (activates isoniazid), produce NIACIN, CORD FACTOR/trehalose mycolate- inhibit mac maturation, SULFATIDE surfarce glycolipids- inhibit phagolysosomal fusion, grows very slowly
    only found in humans, causes TB (fever, night sweat, weight loss, hemoptysis), granulomas and caseation by CD4/macs, reactivation/dissemination from immune suppresion
    ZIEHL-NEELSEN STAIN, 3-6wk culture on LOWENSTEIN-JENSEN MEDIUM
    PPD skin test after 48 hr innoculation, type 4 HSR, not necessarily active TB
    IGRA more specific, fewer false-pos form BCG vax
    tx 6-9mo with isoniazid+rifampin+pyrazinamide, add ethambutol for sever (meningitis)
    prevent with BCG vax with live attenuated Mycobacterium bovis (limits M.tb but doesn't prevent)

  • mycobacterium avium-intracellulare

    slow growing MOTT, causes disseminated dz in immunocompromised, highly abx resistant

    prophylactic azithromycin tx in AIDS

  • mycobacterium kansaii

    slow growing MOTT, causes TB-like dz in immunocompromised, less abx resistant than MAC

  • mycobacterium marinum

    slow growing MOTT, causes swimming pool granuloma/fish tank granuloma (skin lesion)

  • mycobacterium scrofulaceum

    slow growing MOTT, causes scrofula (cervical LAD)

  • mycobacterium fortuitum-chelonei

    fast growing MOTT, causes prosthetic joint/catheter infx, skin infx at puncture wounds, resistant to most TB drugs

  • Mycoplasma pneumoniae

    NO GRAM STAIN (no cell wall), CHOLESTEROL in cell membrane, produces H2O2
    causes atypical pneumonia (insidious, headache, dry cough, patchy/diffuse infiltrate)
    10+ day culture on EATON AGAR, positive IgM COLD AGGLUTININ test
    tx with erythromycin/tetracycline, no PCN b/c no cell wall
