  • Staphylococcus aureus

    G+ cocci in CLUSTERS, CATALASE +, COAGULASE+, beta hemolytic, beta lactamase
    protein A binds Fc-IgG to prevent complement/phagocystosis
    colonizes nose and skin, transmission via hands
    TSS exotoxin= superantigen, enterotoxin= preformed in food, SSS toxin= desmoglein cleaving protease causing skin sloughing
    form ABSCESS WITH PUS
    tx with PCN-G, Nafcillin for Beta-lactamase, vancomycin for MRSA (PBP mutation)
    prevent with cefazolin for surgical wounds, handwashing dec spread

  • staph epidermidis

    G+ cocci in CLUSTERS, CATALASE +, coagulase-, NOVOBIOCIN SENSITIVE, nonhemolytic, beta lactamase
    CLYCOCALXY BIOFILMS on prosthetic implants and catheters, NEONATAL SEPSIS
    tx with vancomycin and rifampin/aminoglycoside

  • staph saprophyticus

    G+ cocci in CLUSTERS, CATALASE +, coagulase-, NOVOBIOCIN RESISTANT nonhemolytic, beta lactamase
    2nd MCC UTI in young women

  • Streptococcus pyogenes

    G+ cocci in CHAINS, catalase -, GROUP A BETA-HEMOLYTIC, BACITRACIN SENSITIVE
    M protein prevents phagocytosis, molecular mimicry
    hylauronidase in cellulitis, erythrogenic toxin (superAg)= scarlet fever rash, streptolysins O and S= hemolysis
    PHARYNGITIS, CELLULITIS
    rheumatic fever after pharyngitis by cross-rxn and ASO
    acute PSGN after pharyngitis/cellulitis by complex deposition and anti-DNaseB
    tx with PCN-G

  • Strep agalactiae

    G+ cocci in CHAINS, catalase -, GROUP B BETA-HEMOLYTIC, BACITRACIN RESISTANT, CAMP TEST+
    NEONATAL MENINGITIS AND SEPSIS
    vaginal flora, transmission during birth
    tx with PCN-G

  • strep pneumoniae

    G+ LANCET cocci in CHAINS, catalase -, ALPHA-HEMOLYTIC, OPTOCHIN SENSITIVE, BILE SOLUBLE, QUELLUNG RXN-POLYSACCHARIDE CAPSULE prevents phagocytosis, IgA PROTEASE allows resp mucosa colonization
    upper resp tract flora
    pneumonia/meningitis in adults, otitis media/sinusitis in kids
    viral resp infx predisposes pneumococcal pneumonia (damaged mucociliary elevator), splenectomy predisposes to sepsis, skull fracture predisposes to meningitis
    tx with PCN-G (no beta-lactamase)
    prevent with 23-valent capsular vax or 13-valent vax coupled to diphtheria toxoid

  • Viridans strep

    G+ cocci in CHAINS, catalase -, ALPHA-HEMOLYTIC, OPTOCHIN RESISTANT, BILE INSOLUBLE
    oral flora
    bacteremia from dental procedure --> heart valve vegetation, tx with PCN-G and aminoglycoside
    prevent with PCN in pts with damaged/prosthethic heart valves undergoings dental work

  • Enterococcus faecalis

    G+ cocci in CHAINS, catalase -, GROUP D GAMMA-HEMOLYTIC, grows in BILE AND 6.5NaCl
    colonic flora
    UTI and biliary tract infx, subacute endocarditis after GI/GU procedure
    VRE causes nosocomial infx, tx with linezolid
    tx with PCN/vancomycin+aminoglycoside
    prevent with PCN and gentamicin in pts with damaged heart valves undergoing GI/GU procedure

  • Strep bovis (Nonenterococcus)

    G+ cocci in CHAINS, catalase -, GAMMA-HEMOLYTIC, grows in BILE
    bacteremia and subacute endocarditis in COLON CA PTS

  • Bacillus anthracis

    G+ ROD, spore-forming, aerobic, NONMOTILE, POLYPEPTIDE D-GLUTAMATE CAPSULE- antiphagocytic
    woolsorter's dz (inhale spores- pulmonary), animal/wool contact (cutaneous- painless necrotic ulcer with black eschar)
    anthrax toxin- edema factor (AC- cAMP), lethal factor (inhibit cell division), protective factor (REQUIRED, entry into cell)
    tx with PCN-G
    vax of protective Ag for high-risk occupations

  • Bacillus cereus

    G+ ROD, spore-forming, aerobic
    food poisoning from rice- spores survive boiling and germinate at warm temp (reheating), emesis in 1-5hr from cereulide toxin, watery diarrhea in 8-18hr
    2 enterotoxins- cholera-like (cAMP), staph-like (superAg)

  • Clostridium tetani

    G+ ROD, spore-forming, ANAEROBIC, tennis-racket shape
    found in soil (traumatic skin breaks)
    exotoxin blocks inhibitory NT release (GABA, glycine) causing tetanus/spastic paralysis, tetanospasmin is a protease
    lock-jaw, risus sardonicus
    Tx with passive immunity/PCN-G/valium(spasmolytic)
    prevent with toxoid vax (DTaP) and booster every 10 yr

  • clostridium bolulinum

    G+ ROD, spore-forming, ANAEROBIC
    preformed toxin in improperly preserved (canned) food and spores in honey
    botulinum toxin is protease inhibiting ACh release --> flaccid paralysis, toxin is heat labile
    tx with antitoxin made in horses and respiratory support

  • clostridium perferinges

    G+ ROD, spore-forming, ANAEROBIC
    myonecrosis from wound contamination with soil/feces --> GAS GANGRENE, food poisoning from ingestion
    ALPHA TOXIN (LECITHINASE) cleaves cell mem, enterotoxin is staph-like superAg
    EGG YOLK AGAR for lecithinase
    tx with PCN-G and debridement of wound

  • clostridium difficile

    G+ ROD, spore-forming, ANAEROBIC
    colon flora, fecal-oral transmission
    pseudomembranous collitis from Abx (suppress nml flora so C.difficile overgrows), exotoxin A/B inhibits GTPases --> depolymerize actin --> apoptosis, toxin found in stool
    tx- metronidazole, no vancomycin b/c of VRE, fecal txp to prevent relapse

  • corynebacterium diphtheriae

    G+ CLUB-SHAPPED ROD in V/L shape (chinese letter with metachromatic granules, NO SPORES, aerobic, BLACK COLONIES on TELLURITE PLATE
    throat flora
    A/B Exotoxin ADP-ribosylates EF2 --> inhibit protein synthesis --> mucosal epithelial cell death --> pseudomembrane in throat
    tx- antitoxin made in horse, PCN-G
    prevent with toxoid vax (DTaP)

  • listeria monocytogenes

    G+ SMALL ROD, NO SPORES, aerobic, facultative intracellular (listeriolysin, avoid AB), actin rockets allow intercellular movement, TUMBLING MOTILITY, LPS
    unpasteurized milk products
    gastroenteritis, meningitis/sepsis in newborns (transmission across placenta), spontaneous abortion in pregnant women
    tx with ampicillin for infants/immunocompromised/elderly

  • Actinomyces israelii

    G+ FILAMENTOUS BRANCHING RODS, anaerobic, SULFUR GRANULES
    mouth flora, aspiration into lungs, retained IUD
    oral/facial abscess with draining sinus tracts
    tx with PCN-G

  • Nocardia asteroides

    G+ FILAMENTOUS BRANCHING RODS, AEROBIC, ACID-FAST- ziehl-neelsen stain
    found in soil, inhaled into lungs
    tx with sulfonamides
