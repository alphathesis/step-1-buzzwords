  • Treponema pallidum

    spirochete, multiplies at site of inoculation and spreads via blood
    STD, causes SYPHILIS (primary painless chancre, secondary palm/sole rash and condylomata lata, tertiary gummas/aortitis/tabes dorsalis/ARGYLL ROBERTSON PUPIL)
    transmission across placenta after 1st trimester (congenital syphilis-Saber shins, saddle nose, CN8 deafness, hutchinson teeth, mulberry molars)
    DARKFIELD MICROSCOPY, VDRL/RPR nonspecific for screening (cardiolipin, false-pos with virus/drugs/rheumatic fever/SLE) and declines with tx, FTA-ABS specific and remains for life
    tx with benzathine PCN-G for primary/secondary, PCN good for all stages, JARISCH-HERXHEIMER RXN when abx started b/c killed bacteria release pyrogens

  • Borrelia burgdorferi

    big spirochete (ANILINE DYE in LIGHT microscopy- Wright or Giemsa), invades skin and spreads via blood
    from white-footed mouse, transmission via IXODES DEER TICK in NE/MW/W USA, tick must feed for 24+ hr to deliver infectious dose, causes LYME DZ (erythema migrans, bilateral facial nerve palsy, cardiac block, monoarthritis or migratory polyarthritis)
    dx with IgM AB
    tx with doxycyline for early, PCN-G for late

  • Leptospira interrogans

    spirochete, DARKFIELD MICROSCOPY not light
    from wild/domestic animals via ANIMAL URINE, causes leptospirosis with flu-like sx/jaundice/photophobia with conjunctival suffusion (initial bacterimic phase, subsequent immunopathologic phase with meningitis)
    SURFERS IN HAWAII
    Weil Dz- jaundice and azotemia with fever/hemorrhage/anemia
    tx with PCN-G, prevent with doxycycline in short-term exposure, vaccinate pets

  • Borrelia recurrentis

    spirochete, rapid antigenic changes from programmed rearrangements of DNA
    transmitted by BODY LOUSE, causes RELAPSING FEVER

  • Chlamydia trachomatis

    OBLIGATE INTRACELLULAR (inactive elementary body is extracellular and infectious, active reticulate body intracellular)
    STD or trasmission from birth canal, causes inclusion conjunctivitis (types A,B,C)/lymphogranuloma venereum (L1/2/3, small painless ulcer --> ulcerating painful inguinal LN)/nongonococcal urethritis/cervicitis/trachoma/reactive arthritis (Reiter)/pneumonia in infants- all D-K
    GIEMSA STAIN, CYTOPLASMIC INCLUSIONS
    tx with tetracycline or macrolide, erythromycine to infected mother to prevent neonatal dz, doxycycline for lymphogranuloma venereum

  • Chlamydia pneumoniae

    OBLIGATE INTRACELLULAR
    causes atypical pneumonia
    tx with tetracycline

  • Chlamydia psittaci

    OBLIGATE INTRACELLULAR
    transmission via aerosol of BIRD FECES
    tx with tetracycline
