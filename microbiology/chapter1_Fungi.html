  • Systemic mycoses

    can cause pneumonia and disseminate
    dimorphic, mold in cold, yeast in heat
    tx with fluconazole for local and amphotericin B for systemic
    can mimic TB granuloma
    no person-to-person

  • Histoplasma capsulatum

    Ohio and Mississippi River Valley, bird/bat droppings in soil
    inhale spores
    yeast grow IN ALVEOLAR MACS, form granuloma
    culture on sabouraud's agar, tuberculate chlamydospores at 25¬∞
    histoplasmin skin test shows previous infx not necessarily active

  • Coccidioides immitis

    SW U.S and Cali, arthrospores inhaled
    mold in cold, SPHERULES WITH ENDOSPORES in body
    dissemination to bone/skin by spherule rupture releasing endospores
    causes pneumonia and meningitis
    culture on sabouraud's agar
    IgM for recent infx, IgG for dissemination
    coccidioidin or spherulin skin test for prior infx
    tx with amphotericin B for disseminated, ketoconazole for local pulm dz

  • Blastomyces dermatitidis

    east of Mississippi River and central america
    yeast form SINGLE BROAD-BASED BUD WITH THICK WALL
    inhale spores/conidia
    abscesses forllowed by granulomas, dissemination to skin/bone
    tx with itraconazole

  • Paracoccidioides brasiliensis

    Latin America
    yeast with multiple buds in SHIPWHEEL formation
    inhale conidia
    culture on sabouraud's agar
    tx with itraconazole

  • Malassezia furfur

    TINEA VERSICOLOR- cutaneous mycoses
    acid from lipid degradation damages melanocyts, hypo/hyperpigmented patches
    hot humid weather
    speghetti and meatball appearance
    tx with miconazole or selenium sulfide

  • Tineae

    pedis/cruris/corporis/capitis/unguium
    pruritic ring SKIN lesions caused by dermatophytes (microsporum, Trichophyton, Epidermophyton- mold that USE KERATIN)
    mold hyphae in KOH prep (NOT DIMORPHIC)
    trichophytin Ag skin test to test cell-mediated immunity competence (not for tinea dx)
    tx with topical miconazole/clotrimazole/tolnaftate, griseofulvin for unguium/capitis
    prevent by dry cool skin

  • Opportunistic mycoses

    immune-suppression causes disseminated disease

  • Candida albicans

    thrush, disseminated candidiasis, chronic mucocutaneous candidiasis
    part of normal flora
    pseudohyphae and hyphae when invassive, germ tubes at body temp
    skin lesions on moisture-damaged, thrush in infants/immunosuppressed/abx, disseminated as endocarditis and endophthalmitis in immunosuppressed/IVDA, chronic mucocutaneous in T-cell defect
    gram positive yeast with psuedohyphae, colonies on sabouraud's agar, germ tube and chlamydospores
    candida skin test used to test cell-mediated immunity
    topical azole for vaginal, fluconazole/caspofungin for thrush, amphotericin B for disseminated, ketoconazole for chronic mucocutaneous
    prevent oral with clotrimazole, prevent disseminated with fluconazole

  • Cryptococcus neoformans

    yeast in pigeon droppings
    flu-like or pneumonia that disseminates to MENINGES via bloodstream, soap bubble lesions in brain
    HEAVILY ENCAPSULATED YEAST with INDIA INK/mucicarmine, LATEX AGGLUTINATION for polysaccharide capsular antigen
    tx with amphotericin B and Flucytosine
    prevented in AIDS with fluconazole

  • Aspergillus fumigatus

    invasive in immunocompromised or CGD, ABPA assn with asthma and CF causing bronchiectasis and eosinophila, Aspergilloma fungus ball in cavities (after TB)
    aflotoxins assn with hepatocellular CA
    mold with SEPTATE HYPHAE BRANCHING AT ACUTE ANGLES
    invades BVs (thrombosis, infarction)
    MYCELIUM on sabouraud's agar, chains of conidia radiating from stalk
    IgG precipitins in aspergilloma, IgE ABs in ABPA
    tx with amphotericin B for invasive, steroids for ABPA

  • Mucor & Rhizopus

    mucomycosis in DKA and leukemia
    typically affect sinuses
    hyphae invade mucosa, prolif in BV walls, penetrate cribiform plate to enter brain
    black necrotic eschar, rhinocerebral and frontal lobe abscesses
    NONSEPTATE HYPHAE BRANCHING AT WIDE ANGLES, MYECELIUM on sabouraud's agar, spres in sporangium
    tx with amphotericin B

  • Sporothrix schenckii

    THORN PRICK, pustule/ulcer with ascending lymphangitis
    DIMORPHIC CIGAR-SHAPED BUDDNG YEAST
    tx with itraconazole or POTASSIUM IODIDE

  • Pneumocystis jirovecii

    Yeast/protazoa
    diffuse interstitial pneumonia (PCP) when CD4<200
    DISC-SHAPED on METHENAMINE SILVER STAIN
    tx with TMP-SMX, pentamidine
    prophylaxis with dapsone, atovaquone
