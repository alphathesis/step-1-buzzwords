  • HERPESVIRUS

    HSV1, HSV2, VZV, EBV, CMV, HHV6, HHV8

  • HSV1

    DS linear DNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    herpes labialis, keratitis, encephalitis
    vesicular lesions on mouth/face, latent in trigeminal ganglia, recurrence from fever/sunlight/stress, disseminates in immunecompromised- temporal lobe encephalitis
    TZANCK SMEAR with multinucleated giant cells with INTRANUCLEAR INCLUSIONS
    tx with acyclovir for encephalitis, trifluorothymidine for keratitis, primary infx and local recurrence self-limited
    acyclovir reduces recurrences

  • HSV2

    DS linear DNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    herpes genitalis, aseptic meningitis, neonatal infx
    STD or birth canal transmission
    vesicular lesions on genitals, latent in lumbar/sacral ganglion, recurrence less severe, asymptomatic shedding
    TZANCK SMEAR with multinucleated giant cells with INTRANUCLEAR INCLUSIONS
    tx with acyclovir for primary/recurrence
    prevent neonatal via C-section

  • VZV

    DS linear DNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    varicella/chickenpox in kids, zoster/shingles in adults
    initial infx in oropharynx, spreads via blood to liver then skin, latent in sensory (dorsal root or trigeminal) ganglia, reactivates as zoster
    CPE in cell culture, multinucleated giant cells in smear, intranuclear inclusions, >4-fold AB titer is dx
    no tx for varicella or zoster, acyclovir to prevent dissemination in immunocompromised
    LIVE-ATTENUATED VACCINE, give acyclovir and VZIG with vax to immunocompromised (passive immunity)

  • CMV

    DS linear DNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    MCC CONGENITAL ABNL, CM inclusion dz in infants, -monospot mononucleosis in trasfusion pts, pneumonia/hepatitis in immunocompromised, retinitis in AIDS
    found in body fluids, initial infx in oropharynx, spreads to organs, lymphocyte rxn, latent in monocytes, disseminated infx in immunocompromised
    CPE in cell culture, OWL EYE NUCLEAR INCLUSIONS
    tx with gancyclovir for pneumonia and retinitis

  • EBV

    DS linear DNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    infectious mononucleosis (fever, HSM, pharyngitis, posterior cervical LAD), assn Burkitt lymphoma/Hodgkin/nasopharyngeal CA
    found in oropharynx and B-cells, initial infx in pharyngeal epithelium (kissing dz), spread to cervical LN, travel via blood to liver/spleen, latent in B cells
    MONOSPOT TEST (heterophil AB to sheep RBC), atypical CD8 T cells on smear
    no tx

  • HHV6

    DS linear DNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    reseola (high fever for days that may cause seizures, then diffuse macular rash), saliva transmission

  • HHV8

    DS linear DNA, icosahedral
    kaposi's sarcoma in AIDS pts
    STD, purple lesions from venous blood
    Spindle cells and RBCs in lesion biopsy

  • HEPADNAVIRUS- HBV

    DS incomplete circular DNA, icosahedral, enveloped, DNAp, reverse transcriptase, surface/core/e antigens, serum has mostly long rods and sperical HBsAg
    blood, birth, sex transmission
    hepatocellular injury from CD8 T cells, Ag-Ab complexes cause arthritis/rash/glomerulonephritis, 5% become chronic carrier state, can lead to chronic hepatitis/cirrhosis/hepatocellular CA (integration of viral DNA)
    HBsAg >6mo means chronic carrier, HBeAg means infectious/transmissible, neither HBsAg or HBsAb means window phase with high anti-HBeAg
    tx with pegylated alpha interferon and lamivudine (RTI) to reduce inflammation from chronic hep B (doesn't cure)
    prevention with HBsAg vaccine or hyperimmune globulins from donors, educate

  • ADENOVIRUS

    DS linear DNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped, 41 serotypes
    respiratory dz (pharyngitis, pneumonia), conjunctivities, enteric causes diarrhea
    transmit via respiratory droplet or iatrogenic, infects epithelium, persistent low grade virus production w/o Sx in the pharynx
    AB titer rise in convalescent phase serum
    LIVE VAX for types 3/4/7 used in the military to prevent pneumonia

  • PARVOVIRUS B19

    SS linear- DNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped
    erythema infectiosum (slapped cheek), aplastic anemia, arthritis, hydrops fetalis
    infects erythroblasts, immune complexes cause rash/arthritis, anemia/CHF/hydrops fetalis in fetus from transplacental transmission- maternal AB protects fetus

  • PAPILLOMA VIRUS

    DS circular DNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped, low risk 6/11, high risk 16/18
    papillomas, condylomata acuminata, assn anus/penis/cervix CA
    STD, E6/E7 proteins inhibit tumor suppressors p53/Rb
    KOILOCYTES in lesions
    tx with podophyllin for genital or liquid nitrogen for skin or salicylic acid for plantar
    CAPSID PROTEIN VAX

  • Smallpox virus

    DS linear DNA, complex, DNA-dpdt RNAp, enveloped
    erradicated due to vax
    infx of upper resp mucosal cells, spread to LN and viremia to liver/spleen then skin (macule, papule, vesicle, pustule, crust)
    CPE in culture, POCKS on CHORIOALLANTOIC membrane, CYTOPLASMIC INCLUSIONS
    LIVE-ATTENUATED VAX, only given to military now

  • Molluscum contagiousum

    DS linear DNA, complex, DNA-dpdt RNAp, enveloped
    pinkish papular skin lesion with umbilicated center, lesions on face around eyes, transmit by drect contact
    tx with cidofovir for extensive lesions in immunocompromised

  • POLYOMAVIRUS- JC, BK

    DS circular DNA, icosahedral
    JC= PML in HIV pts
    BK infects kidney in txp pts

  • REOVIRUS- Rotavirus

    DS linear RNA, icosahedral, double capsid, nonenveloped, RNAp, SEGMENTED, resistant to stomach acid
    gastroenteritis in young kids
    infx limited to small intestine, vilous distruction causes dec Na abs and K loss
    stool detection with ELISA
    LIVE ATTENUATED VAX with most common serotype, LIVE REASSORTANT VAX with 5 strains

  • RETROVIRUS

    HIV, HTLV

  • HIV

    SS+ diploid RNA, complex/conical, RNA-dpdt DNAp (reverse transcriptase), integrase, protease cleaves precursor polypeptides to make functional proteins, tat protein activates viral tcp, env gene for gp120 and gp41- gp120 protein antigenicity changes rapidly, gag gene for p24 capsid, pol for transcriptase/protease/integrase
    requires CD4 and CCR5 to enter cells, kills CD4 T cells, NEF protein is virulence factor that reduces MHC1 to avoid killing by CD8, CD8 is main defense against HIV
    4 phases- flu-like, latent, falling CD4, AIDS crisis
    dx with ELISA screeing then western blot confirmation, viral load using PCR (predicts progression to AIDS), AIDS dx when CD4<200 or PJP
    tx with NRTI (zidovudine, lamivudine), NNRTI (nevirapne, efavirenz), protease inhibitors (indinavir, ritonavir), fusion inhibitor (enfuvirtide- blocks CCR5)
    HAART= 2 NRTI+1 protease inhibitor or 1 NNRTI
    prevent with blood screening, safe sex, AZT to HIV-infected mothers and newborns
    AZT, 3TC and protease inhibitor for needle-stick injury
    no vax

  • HTLV

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, gag/pol/env genes plus regulatory tax/rex
    causes adult T-cell leukemia/lymphoma with malignant transformation of CD4 T cells (tax protein --> IL2 --> growth), causes HTLV-assn myelopathy with demyelination of brain/SC by AI cross-rx or by CD8 T cells
    IVDA, sex, breast feeding transmission
    endemic in southern Florida, eastern S.America, western africa, southern Japan
    use ELISA for anti-HTLV ABs
    no tx, no vax
    prevent by discarding blood with anti-HTLV, condoms, refrain from breast feeding

  • TOGAVIRUS

    Rubella, eastern equine, western equine

  • Rubella

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    German measels (fever, POSTAURICULAR LAD, arthralgia, fine rash) congenital rubella causes cadio and CNS malformation plus BLUEBERRY MUFFIN APPEARANCE due to extramedullary hematopoiesis
    initial infx in nasopharynx, spread to local LN then via blood to skin, rash from viral rep and immune injury, during maternal infx virus rep in placenta (maternal AB protects fetus)
    PCR assay to detect virus, IgG in hemagglutination inhibition test to see if woman is immune, IgM for recent infx
    LIVE ATTENUATED VAX (MMR)

  • Eastern equine encephalitis

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    severe but uncommon encephalitis
    East Coast of USA, transmitted to humans/horses by mosquitos from small wild birds, viremia is low

  • western equine encepalitis

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    transmitted to humans by mosquitos from small wild birds

  • FLAVIVIRUS

    HCV, yellow fever, Dengue, St. Louis encephalitis, West nile virus

  • HCV

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    hep C, assn hepatocellular CA
    blood, sex, fetal transmission
    hepatocellular injury from CD8 T cells, >50% become carrier state predisposed to chronic hep and hepatocellular CA
    tx with pegylated alpha interferon plus ribavirin (mitigate chronic hepatitis) +/- protease inhibitor (boceprevir, telaprevir)
    prevention by discarding blood donations with HCV

  • Yellow fever virus

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    yellow fever (high fever, black vomitus, jaundice) in tropical Africa/S.America
    Jungle fever transmitted to humans by mosquitos from monkeys, Urban fever transmitted by AEDES mosquito human-to-human, high viremia
    LIVE ATTENUATED VAX

  • Dengue virus

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, enveloped
    dengue fever in caribbean, transmitted by AEDES mosquito human-to-human, may have monkey reservoir
    second episode causes hemorrhagic fever

  • St. louis encephalitis, west nile virus

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, transmitted to humans by mosquitos from small wild birds

  • Japanese encephalitis

    SS+ RNA, ICOSAHEDRAL, enveloped
    encephalitis outbreaks in Asia, transmitted to humans by mosquitoes from birds/pigs
    INACTIVATED VAX

  • CORONAVIRUS

    SS+ RNA, helical, enveloped
    common cold and SARS
    infx limited to resp tract mucosal cells, 50% asymptomatic, brief immunity and reinfx

  • HEPEVIRUS- HEV

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped
    fecal oral, no chronic carrier, no cirrhosis, no assn hepatocellular CA
    high mortality in pregnanat women

  • CALCIVIRUS- Norovirus

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped
    gastroenteritis with watery diarrhea
    infx limited to intestinal mucosal cells, many infx asymptomatic, brief immunity with reinfection

  • PICORNAVIRUS

    Poliovirus, Echovirus, Rhinovirus, Coxsackievirus, HAV
    RNA translated into large polypeptite then cleaved into proteins

  • Poliovirus

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped, RNA acts as mRNA and is translated into giant polypeptide with subsequent cleaving to form proteins
    aseptic meningitis>paralytic poliomyelitis, most infx asymptomatic or mild
    fecal oral transmission, viral rep in pharynx/GI, spread to local LN then via blood to CNS, paralysis from anterior horn cell neuron death (LMN)
    virus in spinal fluid means CNS infx, virus in stool means infx but no necessarily dz (found in GI of asymptomatic)
    INACTIVATED SALK VAX or LIVE ATTENUATED SABIN VAX (both induce humore antibody to neutralize virus in blood but oral sabin induces intestinal IgA), reversion of sabin can cause dz

  • Echovirus

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped
    causes aseptic meningitis

  • Rhinovirus

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped, 100+ serotypes, destroyed by stomach acid (no GI replication)
    common cold
    infix of upper resp mucosa and conjunctiva, rep in cold temp of nose

  • Coxsackie

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped
    aseptic meningitis, herpangina, pleurodynia, myocarditis, pericarditis, hand/foot/mouth dz, B4 can cause juvenile DM
    fecal oral, initial infx of oropharynx, main site GI tract, spread via blood
    detected by CPE in culture

  • HAV

    SS+ RNA, icosahedral, nonenveloped
    fecal oral, viral rep in GI, spread to liver during brief viremic period, only acute, not cytopathic for liver, hepatocellular injury by CD8 T cells
    IgM for dx of acute infx
    KILLED VAX
    IG during incubation period to mitigate dz

  • ARENAVIRUS- LCMV, Lassa fever encephalitis

    SS- circular RNA, helical, enveloped, SEGMENTED
    Lassa fever encephalitis from mice

  • BUNYAVIRUS- california encephalitis, Hantavirus

    SS- circular RNA, helical, SEGMENTED
    california encephalitis- transmitted to humans by mosquitos from small wild birds
    Hantavirus- pulmonary sx, deer mice reservoir, inhale dried urine/feces

  • PARAMYXOVIRUS

    Parainfluenza, RSV, Measels, Mumps
    all have F fusion protein causing cells to fuse and form multinucleated cells

  • Parainfluenza

    SS- RNA, helical, enveloped
    bronchiolitis in infants, croup in kids, cold in adults
    infx and death of resp epithelium, no systemic spread
    culture and hemeadsorption, IF for identifying

  • RSV

    SS- RNA, helical, enveloped, FUSION PROTEIN ON SUFACE SPIKES, NO HEMAGGLUTININ
    bronchiolitis/pneumonia in infants!!!, otitis media in kids
    infx of lower resp tract in infants, no systemic spread, MULTINUCLEATED GIANT CELLS caused by viral fusion protein
    tx with aerosolized ribavirin
    passive immunization with palivizumab, handwashing/gloves to prevent nosocomial outbreaks in newborn nursery

  • Measels

    SS- RNA, helical, enveloped
    measles (KOPLIK SPOTS in mouth, descending maculopapular rash, Cough, coryza, conjunctivitis), subacute sclerosing panencephalitis is late complication
    initial infx of upper resp, spread to local LN then via blood to organs maculopapular rash from CD8 response to virus-infected skin vasc endothelial cells, can cause giant cell pneumonia and encephalitis in immunosuppresed
    vit A to prevent severe exfoliative dermatitis
    LIVE ATTENUATED VAX (MMR)

  • Mumps

    SS- RNA, helical, enveloped
    mumps, sterility from bilateral orchitis is rare complication
    initial infx of upper resp, spread to local LN then via blood to organs (PAROTID GLANDS, TESTES, OVARIES, MENNGES, PANCREAS)
    cell culture and hemadsorption
    LIVE ATTENUATED VAX (MMR)

  • ORTHOMYXOVIRUS- influenza

    SS- RNA, helical, enveloped, SEGMENTED (antigenic shift from reassortment), antigenic drift from mutations
    infects resp tract epithelium, transmission via respiratory droplets
    rapid ELISA to detect viral AG in secretions, grown in culture and embryonated eggs, hemadsorptions, hemaglutination for viral entry, neuraminidase for virion release
    tx with neuraminidase inhibitor (oseltamivir, zanamivir), widespread resistance to amantadine/rimantadine
    KILLED VAX of purified HA/NA, or LIVE ATTENUATED VAX that is temp sensitive (replicates in cool nasal passage to induce secretory IgA but not in warm lower resp tract), standard vax has 2 A-strains and 1 B-strain
    H1N1 is swine flue

  • FILOVIRUS- ebola

    SS- RNA, helical, enveloped
    causes hemorrhagic fever with high mortality rate
    human-to-human transmission by body fluids
    EM shows thread-like viruses
    DO NOT CULTURE
    no tx, no vax

  • RHABDOVIRUS- rabies

    SS- RNA, helical, enveloped, BULLET SHAPED
    skunk/raccoon/bat reservoir, transmission via animal bit or bat saliva aerosols
    rabies encephalitis (fever malaise --> agitation, photophobia, hydrophobia --> paralysis, coma, death
    viral rcptr is ACh rcptr, viral rep at bite site, retrograde axonal txp up the nerve to CNS, rep in brain then migration to salivary glands
    NEGRI BODIES in cytoplasmof cerebellum purkinje and hippocampus
    vaccine preexposure
    postexposure tx with would cleaning, passive immunization, INACTIVATED vax made in human cell culture
    vaccinate dogs/cats with killed vax

  • DELTA VIRUS- HDV

    SS- circular RNA, requires HBsAg for protein coat, can only replicate in HBV infected cells
    blood, sex, fetal transmission
    hepatocellular injury from CD8 T cells, chronic carrier state and chronic hepatitis
    tx with pegylated alpha interferon to mitigate sx
    prevent by preventing HBV

  • Prions

    protein only, no nucleic acid, resistant to UV/formaldehyde/heat, pathogenic form induces conformational change in nml form (alpha helix --> beta pleated sheet), protease resistant
    causes sporadic CJD, acquired madcow, inherited GSS, acquired kuru
    aggregation of prion filaments and vacuoles causes SPONGIFORM changes
