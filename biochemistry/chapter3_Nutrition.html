  • VITAMIN DISORDERS

    Fat soluble- ADEK, dpdt on gut and pancreas, malabsorption causes steatorrhea
    water soluble- B1/2/3/5/6/7/9/12 and C, B9 and B12 stored in liver

  • A

    antioxidant, part of retinal, important for differentiation of epithelial cells, prevent squamous metaplasia, tx in measles and ApML and acne/wrinkles
    deficiency- night blindness, dry scaly skin, alopecia, keratomalacia
    excess- arthralgia, scaliness, alopecia, cerebral edema, pseudotumor cerebri, teratogenic

  • B1-thiamine

    TPP is cofactor for PDH, alpha-ketoglutarate DH, transketolase, branched chain ketoacid DH
    deficiency- impaired glucose met = ATP depletion worsened by glucose influsion, alcoholic or malnutrition, dx by inc RBC transketolase activity after B1 admin
    Wernicke-Korsakoff (damage to MD nucl of Thalamus and mammillary bodies- confusion, opthalmoplegia, ataxia, confobulation, amnesia)
    Dry beriberi (polyneuritis, muscle wasting)
    wet beriberi (high output CF, dilated CM, edema)

  • B2-riboflavin

    component of flavins FAD and FMN, cofactors in redox rxn (succinate DH)
    deficiency- cheilosis at corners of mouth, corneal vascularization

  • B3-niacin

    component of NAD+ and NADP+ used in redox rxn, from tryptophan, synthesized from B2 and B6, tx for high VLDL with low HDL
    deficiency- glossitis, pellagra (hartnup dz or malignant carcinoid or isoniazid), 4Ds (diarrhea, dementia, dermatitis, Death)
    excess- flushing, hyperglycemia, hyperuricemia

  • B5-pentothenate

    component of CoA used in acyl transfers and FA synthase
    deficiency- dermatitis, enteritis, alopecia, adrenal insufficiency

  • B6-pyridoxine

    pyridoxal phosphate is cofactor for transmination/decarboxylation/glycogen phosphorylase
    used in synthesis of cystathione, heme, niacin, histamine, NTs
    deficiency- convulsions, hyperirritability, peripheral neuropathy, sideroblastic anemia

  • B7-biotin

    cofactor for carboxylation (pyruvate --> oxaloacetate, acetyl-Coa --> malonyl-CoA, propionyl-coA --> methylmalonyl-coA)
    deficiency- dermatitis, alopecia, enteritis (due to abx or lots of raw egg whites- avidin)

  • B9-folic acid

    THF is coenzyme for methylation and important in DNA/RNA synthesis, abs in jejunum, small stores
    deficiency- macrocytic megaloblastic anemia with inc homocysteine, seen in alcoholic/pregnant/phenytoin/sulfonamides/methotrexate, risk of neural tube defect

  • B12-cobalamin

    cofactor for homocysteine methyltransferase and methymalonyl-CoA mutase, abs in ileum with intrinsic factor, large stores
    deficiency- macrocytic megaloblastic anemia with inc homocysteine and MMA, paresthesias and subacute combined degeration due to abnl myelin, irreversible nerve damage if prolonged

  • C

    antioxidant, reduces iron for abs, hydroxylation of collagen, dopamine beta-hydroxylase to make NE, tx for methemoglobinemia
    deficiency- scurvy (swollen gums, bruising, hemarthrosis, anemia, poor wound healing, corkscrew hair, weakened immune sys)
    excess- NVD, fatigue, calcium oxalate nephrolithiasis, inc risk iron toxicity in predisposed

  • D

    D2 ergo from plant, D3 chole from milk and formed in skin, 25-OH D3 stored, 1,25-OH D3 is active
    inc abs of calcium and phosphate, bone mineralization
    deficiency- rickets in kids, osteomalacia in adulds, hypocalcemic tetany (seen in low sun exposure, pigmented skin, premature, exclusively breastfed)
    excess- hypercalcemia, hypercalcuria, stupor (sarcoidosis)

  • E-tocopherol/tocotrienol

    antioxidant for erythrocytes, enhances anticoag effect of warfarin
    deficiency- hemolytic anemia, acanthocytosis, muscle weakness, posterior column/spinocerebeller demyelination (neuro sx)

  • K

    cofactor for gamma carboxylation of clotting factors II, VII, IX, X, C, S
    deficiency- neonatal hemorrhage with inc PT and inc aPTT but nml bleeding time
    warfarin is vit K antagonist
    not found in breast milk

  • zinc

    formation of zinc finger TF motif
    deficiency- delayed wound healing, hypogonadism, dec adult hair, dysgeusia, anosmia, acrodermatitis enteropathica, predispose to alcoholic cirrhosis

  • Ethanol metabolism

    NAD+ is limiting reagent, alcohol dehydrogenase has 0-order kinetics, inc NADH (lactic acidosis, malate preventing gluconeogenesis, glycerol3phosphate making TGs)
    fomepizol inhibits alcohol dehydrogenase (tx for methanol/ethylene glycol poisoning)
    disulfram inhibits acetaldehyde dehydrogenase (hangover sx)

  • Kwashiorkor malnutrition

    protein malnutrition
    skin leasions, liver malfunction- fatty liver from dec apolipoprotein, edema
    small child with swollen belly

  • Marasmus malnutrition

    total calorie malnutrition
    tissue and muscle wasting, loss of subQ fat, variable edema
