  • codominance

    both alleles contribute
    blood groups A/B/AB, A1T deficiency

  • variable expressivity

    phenotype varies
    varying dz severity
    NF1

  • incomplete penetrance

    not all with mutant genotype show mutant phenotype
    BRCA1

  • pleiotropy

    multiple phenotypic effects
    PKU has light skin, intellectual disability, musty odor

  • anticipation

    inc severity or earlier onset in succeeding generation
    Huntington (trinucleotype repeat expansion)

  • loss of heterozygosity

    two-hit hypothesis (both alleles must be deleted/mutated)
    tumor suppresor genes p53 and Rb

  • dominant negative mutation

    heterozygote makes nonfunctional altered protein that prevents normal protein from functioning
    TF mutation in allosteric site (prevents binding of nml TF)

  • linkage disequilibrium

    alleles at 2 linked loci occur together more often than chance

  • mosaicism

    mitotic errors after fertilization, different cell lines within one person, somatic is in tissues/organs, gonadal is only in egg/sperm
    McCune-Albright syndrome

  • locus heterogeneity

    mutations at different loci produce same phenotype
    albinism

  • allelic heterogeneity

    different mutations in same locus produce same phenotype
    beta thalassemia

  • heteroplasmy

    both nml and mutated mito DNA, variable expression, mitochondrial inherited disease

  • uniparental disomy (UPD)

    2 copies from 1 parent and none from other parent, euploid, usu nml phenotype
    heterodisomy- meiosis I error
    isodisomy- meiosis II error or postzygotic duplication with loss

  • impriting

    methylatoin of one allele at a locus to inactivate
    prader-willi= maternal imprinting of chr 15 with deleted/mutant paternal gene or maternal UPD , hyperphagia, obesity, ID, hypogonadism, hypotonia
    angelman= paternal imprinting of chr 15 with deleted/mutant maternal gene or paternal UPD, inappropriate laughter, seizures, ataxia, severe ID

  • ADPKD

    depression, dementia, chorea, caudate atrophy

  • CF

    GFTR gene mut on chr 7 (delete F508)
    misfolded protein retained in ER --> dec Cl and H2O secretion in lugs, inc Na/H2O reabsorp from inc intracellular Cl --> thick mucus
    inc Cl >60 in sweat, alkalosis and hypokalemia b/c ECF Na/H2O loss with renal H/K wasting
    recurrent pulm infx with psuedomonas, rediculonodular CXR, panc insufficiency, steatorrhea, nasal polyps, meconium ileus, infertility in males (no vas deferens, no sperm)
    tx with N-acetylcysteine to loosen mucus

  • X-LINKED RECESSIVE DZ

    bruton TK agammaglobulinemia, Wiskott-Aldrich, Fabry dz, G6PD deficiency, Ocular albinism, Lesh-Nyhan, muscular dystrophy, Hunter syndrome, hemophilia, ornithine transcarbamylase deficiency

  • Duchenne muscular dystrophy

  • Becker muscular dystrophy
